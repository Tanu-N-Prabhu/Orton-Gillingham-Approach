# Grade 5 Writing – Descriptive Paragraph (Hamburger Method)
**Date:** October 8, 2025  
**Duration:** 1 Hour  
**Focus:** Slow, neat writing + 3–5 detail sentences + clear structure  

---

## Part 1: Warm-Up – Fix & Improve (10 minutes)

Rewrite this sentence neatly, slowly, and correctly:

> yesterday i went to the park it was fun

Tasks:  
- Fix capitalization  
- Add correct punctuation  
- Add at least **one adjective**  
- Rewrite it **neatly once**

---

## Part 2: Main Writing Task – Hamburger Paragraph (30 minutes)

### **Prompt:**  
**“Describe your favorite season of the year.”**

Your paragraph must include:  
- **Intro sentence (top bun)** – Tell which season is your favorite.  
- **3–5 detail sentences (meat)** – Explain *why*. Include:  
  - What you see  
  - What you do  
  - How it feels  
  - Why you enjoy it  
- **Conclusion sentence (bottom bun)** – Wrap up the idea.

## Part 3: Expand the “Meat” (10 minutes)

Expand this short detail sentence into a longer, more descriptive one:

> I like summer.

Write **three improved versions**:  
1. Add adjectives  
2. Add sensory details (what you see, hear, feel)  
3. Add emotion  

Example starter words: *warm, bright, excited, breeze, sunshine, swimming, laughter*

---

## Part 4: Neat Writing Drill (5 minutes)

Copy this sentence **twice**, spacing letters evenly:

> When I write slowly, my sentences become clear and easy to read.

Focus on:  
- Clear letters  
- Straight lines  
- Small spaces between words

---

## Part 5: Sharing & Feedback (5 minutes)

Ask Noah:  
- Does your paragraph have a **beginning, middle, end**?  
- Did you add **at least 3 detail sentences**?  
- Did you write slowly and neatly?  
- Which detail sentence is your strongest?
